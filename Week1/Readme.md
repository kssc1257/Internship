# Week 1 - FFmpeg Basics

## Objective
In this week, I worked on understanding how videos are processed using ffmpeg. The main goal was to convert a video into frames and then reconstruct it back into a video.

---

## Task 1: Extracting Frames
- Downloaded a short YouTube video using yt-dlp
- Used ffmpeg to extract frames from the video

Output:
- Multiple frames were generated from the video
- Some sample images are uploaded in this folder

---

## Task 2: Frames to Video
- Trimmed the video to 1 minute
- Extracted frames at 30 FPS (~1800 frames)
- Recreated the video from those frames

Output:
- Reconstructed video link:
- https://drive.google.com/file/d/1meH27KBlys0epI8_zYVAX_dGpMt43wHj/view?usp=drive_link

---

## Task 3: Adding Audio
- Downloaded a 1-minute audio from Pixabay
- Trimmed it using ffmpeg
- Merged it with the video

Output:
